Who’s this for?

This workshop is designed for companies with little to no internal marketing support. For some of these companies there might be one or two people “assigned” to marketing. Other times we’re talking directly with the owner. And in the B2B world, we’re often working with the business development and/or sales team.

Who this isn’t for:

If your team has a good handle implementing results-based, highly targeted marketing that’s driving positive results, than this workshop may not be a good fit. With that being said, our team works with many large and small organizations to help implement marketing strategies, build and test proof of concept prototypes for product ideas, and lots of other thoughtful design and marketing related projects.

How is it structured?

This workshop starts with an overview of the marketing solutions, platforms, and tactics that make the most sense for your small business. We then move on to specific strategies and tactics that your team can implement and begin testing.

Why do you offer this?

We identified a theme with most small businesses we were working with. They understand that they should be doing some marketing, but are often unsure where to focus, or sometimes, where to start. And to make it trickier, they are often flooded by pitches from marketing vendors and technologies on how to target their potential customers. Many of these businesses can’t necessarily afford outsourcing their marketing, so we put this workshop together to identify what can work for you, and provide the foundations to get started.
